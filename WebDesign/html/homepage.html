About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a software developer for Produce Pro Software in the Chicago area. Recently, I’ve become proficient with web development through my professional work. I have a strong background in low level technologies from my Computer Engineering studies at Purdue.

My Story

Produce Pro

I started working at Produce Pro in July, 2018. This company gave me a great opportunity to get my feet wet in the world of web design. I had no prior web design experience, but I was eager to learn. During my time at Produce Pro I worked on many projects ranging from backend request/response logic to web front end UI design. Produce Pro is a small company of welcoming programmers, and my experience here helped expose me to new technologies and figure what aspect of a full time position were valued to me.

Purdue

My experience at Purdue was a mixed bag of grueling coursework and personal growth. I had initially chosen to attend Purdue because I knew I wanted to pursue a career in STEM, but I didn’t know which direction I wanted to take. Purdue’s first year engineering program helped my find the direction I was looking for. It was at this school that I fell in love with computing technology, and a better group of friends than I ever could have hoped for. I’m grateful for the challenges in my coursework that taught me how to find answers to difficult problems. Whenever I think back to the lessons I learned at this school, I am truly amazed by how much I grew, at Purdue.
